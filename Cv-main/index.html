About

Mostafa Hawash

Mostafa Tolba Hawash (born on January 15, 2004 in Damanhour , Beheira Governorate ) is an Egyptian Author / Writer interested in health, comedian, astronomical, national, archaeological and historical issues, and has many articles, published in national and Arab newspapers , especially in " La Wakt Lel Dayaa " as one of her books. He also had periodical articles in several Egyptian magazines, including a monthly scientific article


Its origins and notable achievements

He Attended El-Safwa Language Schools in Damanhour in the primary and secondary stages. He joined the Faculty of Medicine at Sechenov State University while continuing in the love of writing.
